Overview
	A creepy man that regularly appears to be monitoring you. He speaks with you and it's clear that he knows much more than he lets on. He's never fully understood, and he does things he clearly shouldn't be able to do.

Lore
- He's one of the ancient people of [[Tollwether]]
- He's been designated to watch over the party to ensure things play out how they want
