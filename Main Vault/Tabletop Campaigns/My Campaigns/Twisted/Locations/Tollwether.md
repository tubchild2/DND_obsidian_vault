Local Primordial Granted Abilities (locked by bloodline)
- Ancient, forgotten species
- Their technology can be found, but they cannot
- Legend says they disappeared into another time
- They act as observers and benefactors
- You can find their ruins but you'll never know you're speaking to one


**Locations**
Surface
- Growth
	- Everything in Limina seems to point towards Tollwether
	- This is the source of everything
- Spacetime Anomalies
	- The spacetime ripples have intense psychological side effects
	- The longer you stay here, the less you want to leave
	- It gives you glimpses through space and time
	- It makes you an enlightened weirdo
	- There are unsettling structures here that bend time and space
- The Graveyard
	- The proximity to the pit causes the dead to have their minds linger on temporarily
	- It induces terminal lucidity for an additional day after death
	- Consequently, the region is used as a graveyard, and is riddled with death imagery
	- There are dead people just hanging around
	- No one dares go here
- The Ruins
	- The ancient people of Tollwether were time manipulators
	- There's a lot of disturbing imagery about time and the future
	- They know things no one else can understand
	- With time as a non-factor, structures are strangely large and empty
Caverns
- Dungeon
	- A labyrinth of dungeon rooms that constantly change their layout. It's unimaginably complex, and is riddled with the few nightmarish monsters that managed to escape the pit in ancient times. They're all trapped, and furious. 
- The Seal
	- A large red rock door below the dungeon. It's locked in time, meaning it cannot be changed, and it's the only way to access the Pit.
	- The engravings tell the tale of Pandora's Box.
	- It was sealed by a group consisting of one person of royal descent from every kingdom in [[Limina]]. To break the seal, you need an identical group.
	- All the seal is is an agreement between the people of [[Limina]] and the local Primordial to keep the door shut. If the people of Limina (represented by all of the royalty) decide to open the seal, the local Primordial will stop keeping it shut.
- The Pit
	- There is a bottomless pit that connects to [[the Primordial Deep]]. It is not to be interacted with, as a single Primordial is powerful enough to easily ruin the entire universe.
	- Primordials pass random garbage to try to break the seal, meaning the space beyond the pit is chaotic and dangerous.
