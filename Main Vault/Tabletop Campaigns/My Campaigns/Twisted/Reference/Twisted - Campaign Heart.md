
Overview
- A parallel world that feels alien. Retrofuturistic but ruined tech, unsettling phenomena, weird NPCs, death, and incomprehensible nature. Natural forces in the [[Limina]] manipulate local spacetime, gravity, and flesh.
- An otherworldly conqueror named [[Knell]] has brought the party here by accident. He is slowly working his way through the entirety of the land.
- This universe is used as a dumping ground by several Primordials. Beneath the Land of [[Tollwether]] is a large wormhole that connects to [[the Primordial Deep]]. It's been sealed by a member of every bloodline for centuries.
