#### **THE DREAMER**
The multiverse is the dream of an entity known as the Dreamer
Mental energy is tangible because of this


#### **MENTAL ENERGY, ANTIMATTER, AND NEGATIVE ENERGY**
Mental energy can be used for the creation of matter and energy, at a price. Since matter can neither be created or destroyed, using mental energy to do so created negative energy and/or antimatter to tip the scales to balance. Mental energy can also be used to create antimatter / negative energy, but doing so creates matter / energy. If antimatter contacts matter, or if something with negative energy contacts something with energy, the contact will annihilate both.


#### **THE MULTIVERSE, LIGHT WORLDS, AND DARK WORLDS**
So, when the physical multiverse was born, a equivalent anti-multiverse was born made of an equal amount of antimatter and negative energy. We call these light and dark worlds, and they can both exist within the multiverse. Whenever decisions are made in the multiverse, the timelines split and create new worlds. The law of conversion continues on, creating light or dark variants whenever this happens.

Universes branch in the event of Primordial intervention. That's very important, because that means that there's really only one untouched universe. The rest have insane lore and are completely different.


#### **[[THE PRIMORDIAL DEEP]]**
After the multiverse came [[the Primordial Deep]]. Mental energy produced by the conscious inhabitants of the multiverse flooded into the emptiness and created the Primordials.


#### **PRIMORDIALS**
Each Primordial represents and governs over one distinct concept. Their concepts can be similar, but never identical. The more their concept exists in the universe, the more mental energy will be funneled into them, and the more powerful they'll become. 

Their powers are limited by their concept, and their level of control over it by their concept's prevalence. That means that their powers and abilities really just depend on the individual Primordial. Older, more common concepts are going to yield much power powerful and capable Primordials, typically speaking.

Primordials can travel to any point in the multiverse where their concept exists. When in Light Worlds, Primordials take on physical bodies. When in Dark Worlds, they take on negative bodies. These bodies can be destroyed, and they can be killed. Once they're killed, a new Primordial will be born to take their place. 

Primordials want to propagate their ideas to grow more powerful and to live longer. They often enslave mortals and enforce their concept into their lives to make themselves more powerful. Depending on the Primordial, this can be either a good thing, a bad thing, or a negligible thing. Especially vicious Primordials will occasionally hunt weaker Primordials of compatible concepts to consume their mental energy. Primordials may also prefer the strategy of destroying anything in opposition to their concept to make theirs more prevalent. All in all, they want to spread their concept and to get more powerful.


#### **MAGIC**
Mental energy can be used to will matter and energy into existence. Who can do it and what can be done with it depends largely on the biology, intelligence, skill, and mindset of the person. Magic systems can be created by local Primordials if it aligns with what they want. In at least one case, so many people believed in a magic system that a primordial was birthed from that concept, which came into their universe and made it real.
