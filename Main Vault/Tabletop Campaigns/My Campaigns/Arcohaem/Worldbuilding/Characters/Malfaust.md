### Personality
His philosophy hinges on the natural corruption of humans. He believes that they all inherently want to do bad things but some are just better at hiding it. He believes they're lesser than dragons in every major way. He believes that they're destined to be ruled, and that it's the natural order. He believes that every human, no matter how good, has a price in which they'll break their own rules, especially in the systems they create.

He believes that he is of a higher caliber than humans, and is entitled to rulership. He wants to accumulate all of the treasure in [[Serendipita]] and lord it over the humans.

He is no coward, but he won't die for nothing. If he can escape an encounter that will kill him, he will. 

He's incredibly intelligent, manipulative, and inventive. He often experiments with [[Serendipita|E-Tech]], creating his signature [[Hard-Light]] weapons for his minion; [[Terrik]].

He's horrifically cruel. He'll experiment on his own kind to further his goals. He doesn't care.



### Appearance
A deep crimson creature with tattered wings, large white horns, and small spikes. He's 85 feet long, and 20 feet tall. He has a wingspan of 90 feet, and weighs over 80 tons. He has scales stronger than steel, and is powerful enough to level cities.



### His Plan
**PHASE I**
1. Acquire a servant to act as his public hand.
2. Push [[Arcohaem]] into civil war
3. Dedicate one side's resources to dragon incubation
4. Dedicate one side's resources to weapons development
5. Wait for dragon eggs to hatch, and wait 10 years to allow development
6. Hide and protect female Wyrmlings to ensure egg production
7. Attempt to create dragon embryos artificially
	1. This wing of the laboratory is kept from everyone but [[Atka]], [[Terrik]], [[Kaul]], and the scientists developing it.
	2. It's horrific. He's experimenting on his own kind, often in really cruel and gross ways that puts them in perpetual torment. The lines of tubes filled with embryos are disgusting, and like something out of Metroid. This is to make you hate Malfaust, really hate him.
	3. [[Serendipita|E-Tech]] is capable of aging the dragons in pieces, but isn't powerful for much else. Aging the eggs / embryos directly produces mutant Wyrmlings which are insane and disgusting, so they're ground up and used as food for the new experiments. 
	4. That's where [[Amalgamma]] comes in, a horrific amalgam of young female dragons and [[Serendipita|e-tech]] that can produce dragon eggs.
	5. Once [[Amalgamma]] is capable of producing eggs, phase 2 will be ready.
8. Use the 55 Wyrmlings to seize control of [[Arcohaem]]

1. Lock down control of [[Arcohaem]] with a focus on independent function
2. Stockpile soldiers, weapons, food, money, and any ancient weapons for the coming war
3. Wait additional 5 years to allow Wyrmlings to develop into Young Dragons, to use [[Amalgamma]] to create an army of Wyrmlings, and to ensure foolproof plan
4. Prevent rebellions this time
	1. Destroy communications networks between tribes
	2. Control education systems to prevent military or tactical knowledge
	3. Move weapons away from those not directly benefitting from his rule
	4. Use much stricter policing to prevent *any* rebellious ideas from forming
	5. Instill deep rooted connections and earn the loyalty of everyone in his network
		1. This contributes to his friendly public persona
	6. Use propaganda to present himself as a good person to all of [[Serendipita]]
	7. Turn any potential rebels into warriors for his empire, or kill them
	8. Use extremely strict border control policies
5. Construct overelaborate defenses around [[Arcohaem]]
6. Prepare the world leaders
	1. Use [[Terrik|the Wraith]] to train a legion of followers
	2. The followers will infiltrate and gain the trust of world leaders over these 5 years
	3. Purely good deeds will be done in their honor, presenting Malfaust's network as a force for good

3. Reestablish Draconic Era by instilling local Draconic rulers across the 5 realms
4. Make himself King over all of [[Serendipita]]



### History
12c 117
- Malfaust was Tiamat's main general during the Draconic Era
- When Tiamat fell, he was next in line to the throne
- In fear of the rebellion, he went into hiding deep beneath the planet's surface to grow in power and plot his vengeance

12c 1101
- During the Black Winter, [[Malfaust]] was forced to move deeper into the Volcanic caverns to stay warm. There, he discovered a hidden room from the Draconic age, which contains 55 chromatic dragon eggs preserved in a chemical solution.
- They must have been put there when Tiamat fell. They were ancient, but still mostly intact. With the right incubation equipment, [[Malfaust]] could hypothetically bring them back. He'd begin studying [[Serendipita|ether]], [[Serendipita|E-Tech]], and incubation.
- This process led to the development of [[Hard-Light]] weapons, and  the incubation equipment he needed. However, he needed substantial resources to pull this off. So, he began formulating a plan.

12c 1253
- Malfaust discovered the wounded and exiled [[Terrik|Captain Terrik]] wandering near his lair.
- He lured him in, and questioned him on how he got to the state he was in.
- Malfaust cut him a deal; he'd reestablish his power, and help to bring about a better world with [[Terrik]] at his side. [[Terrik]] would become his right-hand man: his wraith.
- He outfitted [[Terrik]] with a [[Hard-Light]] spear, and instructed him in the primal ways of combat from [[Arcohaem]]'s roots.

12c 1253
- Malfaust uses [[Terrik|the Wraith]] to convince Senator [[Atka]] of [[Frigoshia]] to incubate the dragon eggs, and to declare independence from the Republic. In return, he'd be made King.
- Malfaust uses [[Terrik|the Wraith]] to convince Senator [[Kaul]] to accept the trade deal with Hades, and to begin developing Malfaust's weapons for the Republic's military. In return, he would be made Chancellor.
- Malfaust uses [[Terrik|the Wraith]] to execute the current Chancellor to move [[Kaul]] into position

12c 1255
- Malfaust uses [[Terrik|the Wraith]] to frame the Republic for the assassination of Senator Rimeholt of Nedrena during a peace summons. The already high tensions finally gave way to war between the Republic and the now independent [[Frigoshia]].
- Through blackmail and threats, [[Terrik|the Wraith]] got [[Atka]] the title of King in the new northern Monarchy. 

12c 1266
- The Dragon incubation program has expanded greatly into the volcanic caverns beneath [[Arcohaem]], where Malfaust could directly intervene when he pleased.
- The program was discovered by Republic spies, and the Senate was alerted.
- Malfaust had [[Terrik|the Wraith]] drop the ally act and threaten [[Kaul]] to handle the complication carefully.
- [[Kaul]] and [[Terrik|the Wraith]] established a plan to send the [[PCs A - Arcohaem|Wolves of Arcohaem]] on the mission, scare them off, lie to them, and kill them, to buy them time for a cover-up.
- His plan is nearing completion, with ~50 10yr old wyrmlings under his control, 2 gigantic armies, 2 deeply radicalized and unstable nations, and a gigantic stockpile of [[Serendipita|E-Tech]] weapons.
