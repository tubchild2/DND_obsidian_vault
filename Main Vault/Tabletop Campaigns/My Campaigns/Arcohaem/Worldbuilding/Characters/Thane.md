### Overview
President Thane is the current president of the [[East Arcohaem Trade Union|EATU]], and a prominent [[Normons|Normon]]. He's a diplomat first and foremost, and prefers peaceful negotiations to warfare, but understands what must be done. As a committed [[Normons|Normon]], Thane strongly promotes the cultural values of discipline, family life, cleanliness, and social stability. He views [[Normons|Normonism]] as an important force for strengthening [[Arcohaem]] society during wartime.

### History
Thane was elected in 1260 after President Alba died of old age. His campaign focused on two things: diplomacy and trade. He understood why the war had to continue, but advocated for peaceful negotiations where possible, and the use of trade with Styxas, Fol'Gar'Eth, and Terragon to gain an advantage over the [[Frigoshia|Frigoshians]].

Over the past 6 years, he's brought incredible profits to the [[East Arcohaem Trade Union|EATU]], and has fiercely advocated for their beliefs. Additionally, he's helped to convert thousands of people to [[Normons|Normonism]] during his many tours across [[Arcohaem]].

### Personality
Thane is friendly but professional. He's a very intelligent negotiator, and he doesn't swear or do anything that could conflict with his [[Normons|Normon]] culture.
