### History
12c 1255
	To artificially produce dragon eggs, [[Malfaust]] created Amalgamma.

### Appearance and Function
It's a large number of young female dragons that have been melted together and fused with E-Tech. It's bigger than [[Malfaust]], and lost majority of its color. The entire lower section of the facility *is* Amalgamma, but the heart of the operation is the most important piece. It's a heart of flesh and heads suspended by organs and tubing in the center of a pitch-dark cavern. This part is gigantic, and it's incomprehensible to look at. It periodically lets out a horrific, alien, metallic, discordant wail, and it's constantly twitching violently. 

It's an incredibly powerful funneler, and is aged in pieces and assembled like a machine, meaning it requires a lot of infrastructure to keep it dosile. It needs life-support, ether-draining devices, and powerful sedatives. It it were to wake up, it could kill everything in the caverns except *maybe* [[Malfaust]], given they were close enough to it.
