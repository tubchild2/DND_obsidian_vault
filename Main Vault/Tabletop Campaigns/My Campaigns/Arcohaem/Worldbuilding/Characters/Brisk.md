### Personality
- Devout cultural fanatic
- Obsessed with dragons
- Couldn't give a crap about the industrial revolution if he tried. Whatever allows them to become closer to their draconic ancestors.
- Believes the ends justify the means
	- Willing to use anything to maintain power
	- Doesn't care about lying about his opinions about the industrial revolution if it means helping [[Terrik|the Wraith]] and [[Malfaust]] usher in a better world
	- Doesn't care if his people are massacred if it's worth it in the end
- Ambitious and Flippant
	- Much more openly evil and hateful than [[Kaul]]
	- Kills anyone who threatens his plans
	- Reveals secrets openly with his advisors and just kills whoever has a problem with them because he's in a position of absolute power
	- The people closest to him are terrified of him, and especially terrified of [[Terrik|the Wraith]].
- Incredibly skilled combatant
	- Being raised in [[Frigoshia]] means he was trained since he was 8
	- He preferred funneling to hand to hand combat
	- An incredibly powerful [[Serendipita|ether]] user. Easily the equivalent of level 16 or higher.
	- Doesn't hesitate, and kills incredibly quickly with ambushes of high powered attacks. 
	- Learned some draconic abilities from [[Malfaust]] via [[Terrik|the Wraith]].


### History
- Former [[Frigoshia|Frigoshian]] Senator (now known as Torhild)
- Took a deal to incubate dragons from [[Terrik|the Wraith]]
- Took a deal to push for independence from the Republic
- Became King of [[Frigoshia]]
- Funded an expansive draconic laboratory
