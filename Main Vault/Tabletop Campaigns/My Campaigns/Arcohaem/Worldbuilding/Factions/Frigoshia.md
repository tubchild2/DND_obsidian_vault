### Overview
Founded 12c 118
At war with [[Republic of Arcohaem]] since 12c 1258
Conservative, cultured, and cruel



### Culture
Incredibly conservative. They're prideful, honorable, traditional, and warrior-focused. They have the Unabomber philosophy. Known for being very connected to the land, to their history, and to their ancestors.
- Industrial society replaces the human experience with artificial surrogate experiences.
- Technology forces humans to adapt, not the other way around.
- Technology will inevitably lead to greater suffering, catastrophe, genetic engineering abuses, and the loss of human control.
- Modern individuals are over-socialized, meaning they're conditioned to suppress their natural instincts to conform to society's rules and function like components of a machine.
- The industrial system can't be reformed. They advocate for its complete destruction to allow for a return to pre-industrial, "Frigoshian" ways of life.

Technology

- [[Serendipita|E-Tech]] and industrialization are rejected

**Ceremonies**
- The Binding
	- Strong boys that prove themselves are inducted into the military from the age of 8. They train until adulthood, at which point they undergo a 14 day solo trek from Freyrhage to Brunnborg to prove their manhood. 
	- This rite binds them with nature and with the ways of their ancestors, preparing them to tackle whatever may come their way in the future.
- Sovereignty Day
	- Marks the day that the Northern tribes declared independence and became their own people.
	- Food, games, and remembrance of the former year
	- Doubles as new years.



### Government
Each tribe elects a baron, which oversees local government and military, and reports to the King in Torhild / Frigoshia. The broader military is overseen by [[Brisk]], and his 10 generals.
