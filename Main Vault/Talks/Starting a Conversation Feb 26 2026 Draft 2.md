
### ASSIGNMENT
Informal Witnessing
2 minutes
Create an opportunity to share about the work you do as a Bible teacher
Auxiliary classroom

Love People: Humility: Point 4
	Make clear that the Bible is the source of the truths you share. 
	God's word contains thoughts that touch people's hearts. 
	When we use it, we are building their faith on the right foundation.

### SCRIPT
```
A: Hey Robert, how're you doing?

R: Hey Aiden, I'm doing alright. This coffee is kind of killing my mood though.

A: Oh yeah, I tried it yesterday and it tastes like... bitter rust. I mean get that the boss is trying to save money, but at that point I'd rather have no coffee than bad coffee.

R: Yeah. I might start getting up earlier to get Insomnia coffee in the morning. Have you ever been there? 

A: I haven't but my brother goes there pretty often so I've heard it's good. I only know like 2 coffee places, so I usually just make it at home or just go to Starbucks. The coffee isn't life-changing but the workers are nice and they know me at this point.

R: They know you? How often are you going there?

A: Well, actually, I volunteer my time as a Bible teacher every week. We often take breaks at the one by the Bethany QFC.

R: Huh. I used to study the Bible quite a bit when I was younger but I kind of fell away from it, cause I always felt that they didn't use the Bible very much at my church, which kind of made me question where the information was coming from.

A: Yeah, no that totally makes sense. I'd probably think something similar. That's actually one of the things I do appreciate; we do have study guides, but we actually do use the Bible as the primary source of our teachings because, well, it *is* the foundation of our faith. If you'd be interested I could show you what one of our studies look like.

R: I don't think I'm really interested in that as a whole but it's cool you're using the Bible that much. Oh I think lunch is ending soon, so I've got to get going.

A: Oh yeah I should probably hurry too. See ya Robert!

R: See ya Aiden.
```
