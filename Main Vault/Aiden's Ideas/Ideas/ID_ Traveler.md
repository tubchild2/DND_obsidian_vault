# CHARACTERS
### Carter Hayes
##### Abilities
- Healing Factor. Regenerates everything but his memories. Brain damage causes him to forget everything, including who he is and how he got this way. His intelligence resets to a 7 year old level. Prevents aging.
- Carries his wallet, ID, a wad of cash, and a psychic communication device.
- Portals. Can create two linked gateways between any two points in space and time. Like a muscle; improves in both precision and limits with use. Brain damage resets this back to one 6ft portal per day.
- Given his brain is left alone, he can grow to become incredibly intelligent.

##### Personality
- Incredibly kind, loving, and selfless.
- Bursting with childlike optimism.
- Has no sense of self-preservation due to his unbelievable healing factor.
- Savors everything he has, especially adventuring, food, drink, and friends. He knows he won't have them for long. He's incredibly lonely.
- Has no responsibilities or obligations, and if he does, he'll forget about them next time he dies. This means he's not held back by anything.

##### History
- Has no name or origin. Doesn't know where he came from. The nature of his powers suggest Primordial intervention, but proving that is functionally impossible. Primordials are impossible to find if they don't want to be found. You'd basically have to find them on accident. Also, next to know one even knows about them.


# WORLD
- The universe is teeming with life. Each species is radically different in both physicality and technology. Almost all species are limited to their galaxy or smaller due to the expansive distances required for travel. Earth is left alone due to these travel issues.
- Conceptual demigods called Primordials do exist and can warp spacetime to allow instantaneous travel across the universe. Each seeks to perpetuate and propagate the central concept that gives them form. For example, the primordial of death seeks to destroy life, and everything he constructs is themed around death. Very few know about them, because if they don't want to be found, no one will find them.

### Themes
