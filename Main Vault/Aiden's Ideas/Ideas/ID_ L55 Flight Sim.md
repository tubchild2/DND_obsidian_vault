### Overview
A slow, lore-based game in which you live in a space ship. You've awoken aboard a ship that is on course for a fixed destination. You're completely alone and you don't know where everyone went, but you need to manage your time and the ship or else you'll die.

### Gameplay Loop
**Accelerate**
- The faster you go, the more power you expend
- The slower you go, the longer it takes

**Monitor the Ship**
- Check engine temperature. Slow down if it's too hot, or cool it with water.
- Check water cleanliness. Filter it if it's dirty at the expense of power.
- Check septic capacity. Empty it if it's full at the expense of power
- Check power supply. Slow down if it's running low, or expend water to generate power
- Check radar. Speed up or deactivate the ship if there are nearby anomalies. 
- Check cameras. If you didn't catch the radar, use the cameras to trap and vent anomalies at the cost of air and power. The ship goes dark when they board.
- Check air supply. Sleep to lower air consumption or expend water to produce air (AWG unit)

**Take Care of Yourself**
- Drink water
- Eat food
- Use the bathroom
- Take medicine to cure sickness
- Sleep

**Kill Time**
- Monitor radio for transmissions
- Experiment with the console for lore
- Play minigames
