Set in the Lightning-55 universe

You pilot a Class 2 transport ship. You do deliveries from place to place. You need to take good deals and think about costs ahead of time to stay afloat running the business. If you run out of money and can't afford to keep afloat, you'll die. 


### Visuals
1990s FPS
Rusted 80s retrofuturistic
No touchscreens
Analog everything
Oscilloscopes


### Gameplay
**PRE-FLIGHT**
- Select a destination
- Purchase food, fuel, water, and medicine
- Replenish air, power

**FLIGHT**
- Routine
	- Check engine temperature and cool engine at the cost of water (keep reasonable pace)
	- Check air, fuel, food, water, and medicine, power supply
	- Check water cleanliness and replace filters
	- Check septic tank capacity and empty it at the cost of power (do sparingly)
	- Check radar for nearby ships and enter cloaking mode at the cost of power, or temporarily deactivate the ship at the cost of time.
	- Check security cameras for intruders. Trap them with remote locks, and vent them into space at the cost of air.
- Instructions
	- Time --> Starvation --> Eat --> Expend Food / Time
	- Time --> Dehydration --> Drink --> Expend Water
	- Time --> Suffocation  --> AWG --> Expend Water
	- Time --> Hypothermia --> Expend power on heating
	- Time --> Water Contamination --> Change Filters
	- Time --> Air Contamination --> Get in Vents --> Change Filters
	- Time --> Bathroom --> Septic Overflow --> Empty it
	- Time --> Exhaustion --> Insanity --> Sleep Regularly --> Expend Time
	- Time --> Power Deprivation --> Deactivate Rooms --> Lose Functionality
	- Insanity --> Hallucinations
	- Water Contamination --> Sickness --> Use Medicine
	- Septic Overflow --> Sickness --> Use Medicine
	- Engine Overuse --> Engine Overheating --> Expend water to cool it
	- Radar Detects Ship --> Deactivate Ship --> Wait until ships pass to reactivate
	- Radar Detects Ship --> Enter Cloaking Mode --> Expend extra power
	- Failure to use Radar --> Intruder --> Hide in the vents --> Get to security room --> Trap and Vent --> Expend air
- Additional Ship Details
	- Monitor radio for transmissions
	- Experiment with console for lore
	- Sleep to pass time via alarm clock
