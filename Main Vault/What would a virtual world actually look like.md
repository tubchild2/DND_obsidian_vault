The "space" they occupy exists in RAM and is a product of the game's code. 

Each sentient entity also exists in RAM. They are built of billions of complex parameters that are given to them at birth. The minute they're born, they begin using a complex training algorithm to learn and connect these parameters. 

It'd be like our world, except with no laws of physics, and the ability to discern important data about people by checking or modifying their parameters. Additionally, there's a limited amount of disk space, meaning things need to be deleted before new things can be made. So you could make anything given something of equal complexity is destroyed.

All of these programs would be descendants of a sentient mother algorithm.
