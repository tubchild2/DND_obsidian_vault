**Wade Kyou**
- Charismatic. Con artist. Quick witted. Steals, lies, tricks, and brawls.
- Impulsive. Reckless. Has lots of enemies and burned bridges. Takes risks. Has little restraint. He doesn't always get away, and has several scars (both physical and mental) from his experiences.
- A physical man that appreciates quick pleasures like money and adventure. He has no religion, has no friends or family, has no permanent residence, and has nothing of tangible substance. He has a void where God and love usually go that he's filling with money and excitement; it's not doing a great job.
- Good but selfish. Has a functioning moral compass, but it's more of an honor among thieves type thing. He will double cross and manipulate people for selfish gain if they deserve it. He has lines he won't cross (kids, pets, debt slaves, sexual exploitation, etc.)
- Came from an abusive household that he ran away from. He was homeless for a time, so he learned to take care of himself and improvise. He's afraid of close relationships, especially marriage, and has completely rejected that lifestyle because of his upbringing and its effects on him. Was found and taken by social services and adopted by a different family for the rest of his childhood.
Inspired by:
- Nathan Drake (charismatic, impulsive, thieves' code, lines he won't cross)
- Jayne Cobb (physical man, double crosses if deserved)
- Frank Abagnale Jr. (clever, con man)
