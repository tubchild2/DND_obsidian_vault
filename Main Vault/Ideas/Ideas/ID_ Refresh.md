
### Overview
A group of people find themselves inexplicably stuck in a timeloop. Every day, they wake up, endure an insane scenario, and then die. Each day, the scenario changes wildly. Their skills and memories persist, but they're fuzzy.

The place they're in changes somewhat with each iteration but is the same place conceptually each time. There's no memorizing the layout. 

The tone is light and comedic. The characters treat the situations seriously, but the situations are insane and occasionally dark. 

They get to learn a lot about the side characters who are often found in the same place as them. This allows the NPCs to have Majora's Mask levels of depth through each iteration, while also allowing them to be hurt and die repeatedly.
