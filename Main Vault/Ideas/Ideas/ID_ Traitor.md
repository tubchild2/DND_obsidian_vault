**SET UP**
In the year 2000, Federation time enforcers need to travel back in time to destroy a dimensional rift with a special device
- One of them has a card that represents the dimensional rift detonator

The planet is isolating, unknowable, and dread inducing. Each player has knowledge that is non-transferrable, but they have to split up, meaning they need to split up but keep in contact via radios
- Hand out information guides for their characters that they need to use as references that can't be memorized. Small paper booklets.

They will be travelling two months into the past. To avoid paradoxes, always follow these rules of time travel:
1. Never travel to anywhere you've been previously, and avoid other instances of yourself at all costs. Any action that influences your past self could alter your history and lead you to not making said action, creating a paradox.
2. Do not share or abuse information about the future for causes not greenlit by the Federation.
3. If captured, resort to self immolation to prevent catastrophe.

There is a parasite called the Jagged Mucous Leech. It hides in dark corners and jumps onto the face. It attempts to enter through the nose, ears, or mouth, and then it bores itself into the brain. Once attached, it quickly paralyzes and overtakes the host. It creates offspring that can be used to infect others. The host now wants to hide its true intentions and infect those around it. How it does this is up to the host. Once the body is drained of its natural resources and cannot be used to infect anymore people, the host dies, and the leech moves on.

There's also a creature known as the Harvestman Wolf, a tall creature that, interestingly, only attacks groups of people. The fewer targets there are, the less interested it becomes. It is unknown why.


**MIDDLE**
They arrive on the planet and thinks are unknowable fast. There appears to have been extensive federation testing on this planet, as they're exploring a semi-organic planet. As they continue to explore, they encounter some dead federation soldiers crawling with leeches. Interestingly enough, all of their IDs match. 

They need to split up because the Harvestman Wolf is near and there are tasks they need to do
Separate the players
Use phones to communicate to build immersion

Attempt to infect all of them while isolated

Bring them back together
Let them kill each other
