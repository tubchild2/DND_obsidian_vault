
### Overview
Set in Rain City.
The tone is retro cyberpunk noir. Streets are tense, but culture, music, and art thrive. 
Jazz, smoke, graffiti, underground arts.
Stay serious most of the time, stay in character, keep it clean and PG


### Rain City
- Sprawling Detroit metropolis in 2193 / retro cyberpunk city
- Perpetually raining and perpetually night
- Government is nonexistent, corporations run everything.
- Inequality, crime, danger


### History
2033
- United States begins civil war
- Southern states rebel because of federal leadership after assassination of President Atticus Castor
- The New Confederacy (south), Greater Cascadia (northwest), Neo Northern Republic (north)
- War raged across the continent which destroyed the landscape

2039
- NNR won, but infrastructure was destroyed

2040
- United States destroyed by Civil War
- Detroit became the only viable metropolitan hub for rebuilding civilization
- From 2040-2050, Detroit entered the "Blue Bubble," marked by rapid economic growth, innovation, and an influx of immigrants.
- The Third Compound was discovered, a blue material with mysterious properties

2050
- The Moon Train project showcased the city's technological resurgence. 
- The city developed a blue tint
- Neons are humans altered by the Third Compound, and they're starting to pop up around this time.

2080
- By 2080, Detroit collapsed into Corporate controlled districts. 
	- PentaCorp
	- Pharmatek
	- Netflix-Warner Bros. Discovery-Disney-Universal-Paramount Global Media Conglomerate
- Crime, espionage, and corruption were commonplace
