Name: Johnny Hayes
Species: Halfling
Class: Bard
Background: Wayfarer

##### **Personality**
An illiterate dyscalculic man with a terrible memory, no plan, and no insight. He's insecure because of his mental challenges and views everyone else as literally superior. He's a loving, encouraging, apologetic, forgiving, and brave knight in shining armor. He is commonly underestimated for his complete lack of... well most things, but he can entertain people and can be funny (intentionally or not). He likes the color green, chicken fingers, apple juice, and Weird Al Yankovic.

Goals
- Prove himself
- Help people

### **APPEARANCE**
A taller, tubbier pale man with disheveled medium length black messy hair.
He wears a stained white undershirt, a green overcoat, black pants, and brown boots.
He has a friendly look to him.
Always carrying his guitar.



### **BACKSTORY**
**EARLY YEARS**
- Born with a mental disability that runs in his family.
- He has trouble learning things. He can think clearly, but he has a terrible memory for anything complicated.
- Developed a close relationship to his cousin Carter, who had a similar mental disability.

**ADULT LIFE**
- His life has been one disappointment after another.
- His cousin Carter went missing exploring a mountain that emerged after an earthquake. This disappearance hit him pretty hard.

**TODAY**
- He's been living off of pudding, ramen, and lake water for the past few months. He lives in a studio apartment that's comically small and dysfunctional. He's kept it as clean as he could, but it's naturally just gross.
- He's broke and working as a bartender, where he's regularly harassed by drunk customers. He's "friends" with the other employees, but they don't like him at all and regularly bully him.
- After leaving work, he found a dead body in the dumpster behind the bar. He contacted the police and reported it. No other evidence was found, and he absolutely bombed the interrogations, which made him look incredibly guilty. He was found guilty and sentenced to life in prison.

**LITTLE MEMORIES**
- Tried to write an apology letter when he was in school and it was unintelligible, but he made a good friend that day because they appreciated it.
- His mom and dad are incredibly sweet and they would make him and Carter chicken fingers and apple juice whenever they fought.
- He once performed at an open mic and did terribly but he still got a handful of claps.
- He once planned a costume party at his house and only two people showed up, one of which was Carter, but it was still fun.
