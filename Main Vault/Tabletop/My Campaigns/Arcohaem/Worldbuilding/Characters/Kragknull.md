### Personality
- Kragknull is a war hardened but friendly man
- He's seen a lot, and he values close friends
- He's generally stoic, but he warms up around the [[PCs A - Arcohaem|Wolves of Arcohaem]], who he has a soft spot for.
- His loyalties lie in what he knows. If he knows he can trust you, or if he knows he can trust an organization, he'll remain loyal.

- He likes the Wolves and is facing backlash because he defended the Chancellor's decision to choose them.
- Refers to them by their names instead of by their unit numbers.


### History
Joined the [[Republic of Arcohaem]]'s military in 1248 out of genuine interest
In 1256, he ranked up to captain, then major, where he founded the strike team "The [[PCs A - Arcohaem|Wolves of Arcohaem]]."
In 1261, he was promoted to Commander, and his fellow wolf [[Finn]] finished his service and became a Senator after his father's passing created an opening for him. 

As a Commander, Kragknull was placed in charge of the new [[PCs A - Arcohaem|Wolves of Arcohaem]], who he developed a close attachment to.
