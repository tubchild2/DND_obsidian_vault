### History
His father was a Senator, so he was firmly established in Republic politics from a young age, where he befriended Lord Phenstinor of [[House Havran]]. Became a common military advisor during the early skirmishes. Eventually, learned to funnel [[Serendipita|ether]] himself, and became a fairly advanced [[Serendipita|Ether]] user.

Skipped straight to the rank of Major, and was placed on the [[PCs A - Arcohaem|Wolves of Arcohaem]]. He really enjoyed being on the team and developed a close friendship with [[Kragknull]]. In 1261, Finn's father died, and he was set to inherit the role of Senator. [[Kragknull]] was also promoted to Commander this year.

During his service, he befriended [[Thane|President Thane]], who he appreciated for his genuinely solid values as a person, not just as a politician. He isn't a [[Normons|Normon]] himself, but he gets where they're coming from.
