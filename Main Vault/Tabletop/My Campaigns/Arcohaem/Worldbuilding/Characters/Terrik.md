**-- TRAITS --**
**Legendary Resistance (3/Day)**
	If the Wraith fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.

**Deflect Attack (Reaction) (3/Day)**
	When the Wraith is hit by a melee attack, he can expend his reaction to reduce the damage by 1d10 + 13. If this reduced the damage to 0, he can immediately make one unarmed strike against the attacker as part of the same reaction.
	When the Wraith is hit by an [[Serendipita|ether]] attack, he can expend his reaction to reduce the damage by 1d10 + 20. If this reduced the damage to 0, he can immediately use his [[Hard-Light]] spear to deflect the attack back to the attack. The attacker must repeat any necessary saving throws or take half of the damage.  

**Action Surge (1/Short Rest)**
	On his turn, the Wraith can take an additional action

**Improved Critical**
	The Wraith scores a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20

**Sneak Attack**
	If the Wraith has advantage on an attack roll due to a target being unaware of his presence, he can add an additional 6d6 damage to a damage roll made on a success.

**Lightweight**
	The Wraith cannot take fall damage.



---
**-- ACTIONS --**
**Multiattack**
	The Wraith makes two attacks using any combination of his [[hard-light]] spear and claw gauntlets. 
	The Wraith can forgo the use of weapons and make a combination of 4 unarmed strikes / throws.
	Using Shield Breaker expends all 4 unarmed strikes. 
	Using Grapple expends all 4 unarmed strikes. 

**[[Hard-Light]] Spear**
	Melee Weapon. +13 to hit, reach 10ft., one target. 
	Hit: 20 (2d12 + 7) piercing damage plus 9 (2d8) fire damage.

**Claw Gauntlets**
	Melee Weapon. +13 to hit, reach 5ft., one target.
	Hit: 23 (3d8 + 10) slashing damage. 

**Unarmed Strike**
	Melee Weapon. +13 to hit, reach 5ft., one target.
	Hit: 12 (1d10 + 7) bludgeoning damage.

**Shield Breaker**
	The Wraith repeatedly bashes, claws, and rips a creature's shield. The creature must succeed a DEX save of 26 or their shield will be permanently shattered or stolen by the Wraith (Wraith's choice). 

**Throw**
	One creature within 5ft. must succeed a DC 19 Strength or Dexterity saving throw (target's choice) or be hurled 40ft. in a straight line, landing prone and taking 12 (1d10 + 7) bludgeoning damage. If a target strikes a solid object or creature during this movement, both take 7 (2d6) bludgeoning damage. 

**Strangle**
	One creature within 5ft. must succeed a DC 19 Strength or Dexterity saving throw (target's choice) or be grappled (escape DC 19). While grappled this way, the creature is restrained and takes 23 (3d8 + 10) bludgeoning damage at the start of each of it's turns as the Wraith chokes it. The Wraith can maintain this grapple while moving but cannot attack for the rest of his turn.

**-- BONUS ACTIONS --**
**Bonus Claw**
	The Wraith makes one additional Claw attack

**Bonus Unarmed Strikes (x2)**
	The Wraith makes two additional unarmed strikes

**Shadow Step**
	While in dim light, darkness, or while hidden, the Wraith can teleport up to 60ft. to an unoccupied space he can see that is also in dim light, darkness, or is unobserved. He is automatically hidden after doing this. 

**Vanish**
	While in dim light or darkness, the Wraith can take the Hide action, regardless of whether he's observed or not. If unobserved, the Wraith can take the Hide action as a bonus action regardless of the light level.



---
**-- LEGENDARY ACTIONS --**
The Wraith can take 3 Legendary Actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time, and only at the end of another creature's turn. He regains spent legendary actions at the start of his turn.

**Strike (1 action)**
	The Wraith makes one Unarmed Strike, Claw Attack, or attack with his [[Hard-Light]] Spear.

**Lunge (1 action)**
	The Wraith moves up to 25ft. without provoking opportunity attacks, and can make one unarmed strike at a creature within range. 

**Throw (2 actions)**
	The Wraith uses his Throw action against one creature within reach

**Teleport (3 actions)**
	The Wraith teleports up to 60ft to an unoccupied space he can see. He immediately takes the Hide action.

**Group Combat**
	Attacks through rapid intense ambushes until hit. Then, hides ends combat. Uses down time to intimidate, plan, and sabotage. Targets healers first. Hit people with the remnants of their dead party members. He is a cold and calculated killer, but now he's also toying with the group.  

**Single Combat**
	Relentlessly attacks and expends all legendary actions. Does not retreat or waste time. He is cold and calculated. He may toy with his prey, but he usually will just try and finish the mission instead of taking any unnecessary risks.

### Overview
Terrik Malvek was the greatest soldier in all of [[Arcohaem]]. While trying to uproot corruption by killing criminals, he became corrupt himself, and was scarred and exiled. He became the Wraith; the hand of [[Malfaust]]. He is known only through rumors as an unstoppable monster that kills without resistance. He is to be feared.


### History (overview)
- Came from dirt
- Dedicated his life to military service
- Overcame his insecurity and depression and became a war hero
- After being insulted, he trespassed and killed a man in self-defense
- He continued pursuing those he thought were corrupt
- His ego swelled to ridiculous proportions 
- One of his victims' brothers killed his girlfriend, so Terrik murdered him publicly
- He was arrested, stripped of his titles, and his work was discovered
- He was set to be executed, but he survived with severe scars and went into exile
- He gained access to [[Serendipita|Ether]]
- [[Malfaust]] convinced him to join him
- He became the Wraith and assisted him with anything he wanted
- His persona as "Lord Commander Galen, leader of the Royal Guard" is incredibly mysterious. He's so threatening, quiet, and stealthy that no has really had a conversation with him, making him appear to have no history. He just appeared and asserted himself. 



### Personality

	- Killing makes him feel powerful
	- Thinks himself something of a moral arbiter due to [[Malfaust]]
- Pessimist

	- Force is the only way to create peace
- Psychopath
	- Kills people who deserve it or if he has something to gain from it
	- Feels no remorse
- Relentless
	- Doesn't sleep due to [[Serendipita|E-Tech]] mods he built
- Intelligent
	- Thinks on his feet
	- Doesn't buy lies
	- Masterful deduction
	- Sets traps and negotiates instead of just fighting
- Independent
	- Uses the Republic royal guard when he needs to
	- Follows his own path
	- Terrifies most people
- Charming
	- Can turn on the charm when needed
	- Usually doesn't
