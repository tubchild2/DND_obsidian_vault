A network of drug dealers. They purchase / manufacture drugs and illegal substances. 
Many of [[Arcohaem]]'s elite on both sides use drugs. 

Led by Bjorn Halvyr and his family. 


Bellatoxine
	Street Name: Nightshade
	Source: the distilled alkaloid extract of the nightshade berry
	Effect: 
		- Inflicts poison after 2 minutes
		- Causes convulsions after 30 minutes
		- Stops the heart after 60 minutes
	History: 
		- Used by assassins as a nearly untraceable poison
		- Early versions were crude
		- Modern Bellatoxine is refined into a stable toxin
		- Illegal in most cases
		- Associated with high-profile murders

Aberotene
	Street Name: Berserk
	Source: synthetic adrenal and limbic-system stimulant derived from fungal neurotoxins
	Effect: 
		- Aggression
		- Delirium --> Insanity
		- Hyperactivity --> Exhaustion / Coma 
		- Causes psychosis pretty quickly
	History: 
		- Produced to attempt to suppress fear in infantry
		- Test subjects became violent and uncontrollable within hours
		- Criminal groups recreated the formula

Paleamphetamine
	Street Name: Ghoul
	Source: modified amphetamine made in a laboratory
	Effect: 
		- Renowned for being insanely addictive
		- Weight loss
		- Restlessness
		- Hyperactivity
		- Strength
		- Pale skin
		- Loss of appetite --> starvation
	History: 
		- Began as a black-market performance enhancer for laborers and pit fighters
		- Long-term users developed severe malnutrition and organ failure

Tazoverine
	Street Name: Hush (powder) / Waxy Gas (gaseous)
	Source: Nerve stimulant
	Effect: 
		- Euphoria 
		- Reduces pain. 
		- Makes you feel like you're lighter. 
		- Can cause psychosis with prolonged usage. 
		- Highly addictive
	History: 
		- Developed as a battlefield painkiller
		- Allowed injured troops to continue moving despite catastrophic injuries
		- Civilian versions spread after military supplies were stolen
		- Repeated use slowly damaged sensory processing in the brain

Umbradoxine
	Street Name: Uberdust / Rattle
	Source: cheap psychedelic produced from solvent residues and fungal cultures
	Effect:  
		- Addiction
		- Random effect (1d4)
			- Euphoria
			- Nausea
			- Confusion
			- Paranoia
	History: 
		- Created accidentally in illegal labs
		- Because production is inconsistent, every batch behaves differently
		- Became common among the poor for being cheaper than alcohol

Lumethrazine-9
	Street Name: Redline
	Source: specialized chemistry [[Serendipita|E-Tech]]
	Effect: 
		- Strength
		- Focus
		- Hyperactivity
		- Aggression
		- Causes the user's eyes to glow a deep low red
		- Highly addictive
	History: 
		- Lumen is a synthetic drug produced with [[Serendipita|E-Tech]] by the Republic government for combat purposes. 
		- Known for making people aggressive and incapable of sleeping. 
		- Expensive: only used by the ultra-wealthy, criminals, and some fighters
