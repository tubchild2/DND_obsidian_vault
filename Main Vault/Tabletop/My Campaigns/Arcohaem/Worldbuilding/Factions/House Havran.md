### Overview
Noble house within the ruling class of [[Hephaestus]]. They promote the legend that centuries ago their bloodline was touched by a dragon, hence why the often develop Draconic [[Serendipita|Ether]] potency. It's partially true; to some extent, a lot of [[Arcohaem]] is partially related to the metallic dragons; they have a higher concentration.

To receive the title of Lord or Lady, you need to be the children of the oldest members of their generation, or acquire it through marriage, or acquire it through a substantial government title. Only the heir has the option to choose their bride, otherwise it's arranged or politically motivated.
