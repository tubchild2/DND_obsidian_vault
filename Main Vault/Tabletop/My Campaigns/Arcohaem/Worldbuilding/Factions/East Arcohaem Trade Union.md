### Overview
The alliance of the eastern tribes of [[Arcohaem]]. They formed to protect their interests when the port city Lemnaeus became particularly profitable. They were concerned the larger Republic would vote against their interests, so they unified to stand for issues as one unit.


### Structure
Each senator from the eastern tribes can petition to be granted a seat in the EATU. 
Once approved, they can participate in the biweekly meetings to discuss the future of the eastern tribes.
The union meetings are overseen by a President, who is elected at the start of each decade.
The president has a seat on the Republic senate, and acts as a senator and representative.
If a president dies or is impeached, the Vice President takes over until a new President is elected.
Every senator from the eastern tribes has elected to join the alliance as a protection, or out of pressure from other [[Normons]].
During their meetings, they decide what the Senators should collectively vote for in upcoming Senate hearings to protect the interests of the eastern tribes.


### History
Established in 12c 733 by President Josulf Smithossn. They formed to ensure their powerful trade routes remained intact and that they were able to control their interests better

They created the [[Normons|Normon]] culture to reflect the ideal values of [[Arcohaem|Arcohaemian]] citizens, which became highly proselytized and found particular success in the southern tribes.
