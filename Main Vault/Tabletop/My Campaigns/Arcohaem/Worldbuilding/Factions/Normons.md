### Overview
Though it resembles religion in structure and community organization, Normons do not worship gods. Instead, their belief system is rooted in the theoretical ideal behavior of an [[Arcohaem|Arcohaemian]] citizen. It emphasizes productivity, cleanliness, family life, and collective prosperity.

They're stereotyped as extremely polite, clean, and family centered people who talk nonstop about their beliefs. It's more complicated than that, but that's the gist of it.


### Beliefs
- High society requires large families and constant population growth. Children are the greatest contribution one can make to the nation.
- This philosophy reflects the most efficient and fulfilling lifestyle. Don't condemn outsiders, but rather indoctrinate them through proselytizing. Politely promote their way of life to others.
- Joy comes from simplicity; farming, cooking, working, family.
- Be diplomatic; resolve problems peacefully, and through trade.
- Remain clean in all things, including in what you say.


### History
Created by Josulf Smithossn in 738 following his creation of the [[East Arcohaem Trade Union|EATU]].
Made to represent the theoretical ideal values for an [[Arcohaem]] citizen.
Through evangelism, Normonism spread shockingly well to the southern tribes.
Were primarily beet farmers. In the south, they still are.
