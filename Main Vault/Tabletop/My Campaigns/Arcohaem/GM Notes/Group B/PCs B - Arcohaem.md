### History
See [[PCs A - Arcohaem]]

The [[PCs A - Arcohaem|Wolves of Arcohaem]] were founded in 1256, where they were a party of 5 led by [[Kragknull|Commander Kragknull]]
- [[Kragknull]]
- [[Finn]]
- Fire Genasi Sorcerer named Vaesha. Hotheaded pyromaniac. She got bored easily and was obsessed with fire. 
- Aarakocra Fighter named Arrius. A noble and deeply loyal fighter that inspired those around him to keep going. 
- Thri-Kreen Bard named Rikkix. An incredibly happy and kind guy. He kept people's spirits high and helped them to sweet-talk their way out of trouble.

In 1261, [[Kragknull]] and [[Finn]] retired. This is when the new recruits joined. Over the next 2 years, the 3 remaining original members perished or were lost.
- Vaesha died in a a premature detonation of a military explosive while attempting to collapse a [[Frigoshia|Frigoshian]] fortress.
- During the battle of Kedrik, Arrius gave his life to save a family of Harengon from a [[Frigoshia|Frigoshian]] mage. 
- Rikkix was separated during a blizzard, and hasn't been found. 

Past Missions:
- Duel with the Red Knight. The Red Knight is a mysterious third-party that roams the [[Arcohaem|Arcohaemian]] plains searching for murderers. He's slaughtered countless camps of [[Frigoshia|Frigoshian]] and Republic soldiers. Back when Arrius was still in the party, he had an encounter with him. The Red Knight created a wall of fire around them and challenged Arrius to a duel. Arrius outfought him 3 times, so the Red Knight decided to let him go. He was never seen by the Wolves again. Legends say he was cursed with more power than he can ever use, so now he roams as a symbol of power and justice. A mythological symbol of the faults in both sides of the war.

### Xavi
Name: Lord Lukas San [[House Havran|Havran]]
Species: Human
Class: Conduit (Sorcerer)
Background: Noble

Personality: 
- Manipulative. He treats social interactions like a chessboard.
- Arrogant. Reached the rank of Major through raw merit and survival, giving him a quiet disdain for those who rely on their family name rather than proven strength.
- Extreme Loyalty. Stems from the trauma he experienced, but he'll never leave a man behind. Extremely loyal to the Republic, and to his family.
- Conflicted Resentment. He's bitter about his family, and yet his primary drive is still a deep-seated desire for them to finally recognize and love him.
- Composed and Observant. Possessing a cold, intense focus that allows him to read people and tactical situations with high precision.

Goal: he's determined to prove that his achievements are the result of his own strength, not simply the name he was born with.

Backstory: 
- [[House Havran]] is a wealthy and powerful noble family based in the capital city of [[Hephaestus]]. Their family makes up a fifth of [[Hephaestus]]' senate representation. For centuries they've promoted an old legend that claims their bloodline was once touched by a dragon. Some of the family members occasionally awaken Draconic [[Serendipita|Ether]], becoming conduits through which [[Serendipita|Ether]] manifests in different Draconic forms. 
- Lukas is the fifth heir to the throne of [[House Havran]]; fifth of nine children born to [[House Havran]]. He struggled to master his [[Serendipita|Ether]] abilities; his control was inconsistent and unreliable, making him feel out of place and overshadowed.
- Frustrated, and with a bleak outlook on his future, Lukas left home at 15 to bring honor to his family by enlisting Republic military. He lied about his age, and during his first battle, his battalion was left alone. Then, in the middle of the night, they were ambushed by a shadowy creature with red eyes, horns, and claws. Lukas failed to defend himself, or any of his companions. He barely survived, and he watched as his commander was stabbed in the neck by the creature, now wielding a [[Frigoshia|Frigoshian]] dagger. In that moment, Lukas finally flooded and gained control over his [[Serendipita|Ether]]. He couldn't save his unit, and the guilt still haunts him. He keeps the dagger as a reminder of his guilt, and as a reminder of the monstrosity he's up against. The image of that thing will haunt him for the rest of his life, as will the fact that he's one of the few people to ever see [[Terrik|the Wraith]] and live, and no one will believe him. 
- Over the next 5 years, Lukas learned to control and strengthen the [[Serendipita|Ether]] flowing through him, becoming one of the strongest funnelers in the [[House Havran|Havran]] bloodline. He earned the rank of Major, and he was placed on the [[PCs A - Arcohaem|Wolves of Arcohaem]]. He's hidden his true identity from most people.
- Now 25 years old, he continues to serve the Republic.
- Iona reminds Lukas of his younger brother Akarian. They're very close, because they both understand how it feels to be thrown into situations beyond their control. They view each other as siblings. Iona is the only person who knows Lukas' true identity.

Additional Notes
- Lukas knows [[Finn|Senator Finn]], because his Uncle Phenstinor and him were friends. 
- They weren't close, but he knows who he is, and he knows that him and [[Kragknull]] used to be on the [[PCs A - Arcohaem|Wolves of Arcohaem]].

Theme: We Move Lightly
