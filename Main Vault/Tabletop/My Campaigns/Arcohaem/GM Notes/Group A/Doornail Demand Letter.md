You unlawfully restrained Captain Ren McShill, a now prosecuted drug dealer, by binding him to the figurehead of a sea vessel and exposing him to arctic waters for 5 days. You took his boat, and his crew, against their will. McShill suffered documented injuries including but not limited to severe hypothermia, acute diaper rash, and profound emotional distress. Moreover, said conduct has caused lasting damage to McShill’s reputation and standing among his peers, from which he is yet to recover.

3. **January 27, 1266 – Abandonment of Duty and Complicity in Murder**

You became aware that the population of Freyrhage was in danger of imminent invasion from a Frigoshian threat they posed little chance of defeating on their own. Despite this knowledge you abandoned this settlement and encouraged your companions to do likewise, all while under the influence of Blubberbutter Beer. Furthermore, you were complicit in the killing of a captured soldier you believed to be innocent, solely in furtherance of your personal mission objectives.
