**General Information**
- Abstract creatures native to [[Mythraama]].
- Extremely [[Ether C1]] potent, due to being Abstract and made of [[Ether C1]]. 
- Primordials think each other into existence.
- Each Primordial represents a given concept (or thesis). Their bodies are made of [[Ether C1]], with each particle storing potential petabytes of information. Their thesis is the sum total of all of it. It's decided at birth and cannot be changed due to ideological magnetism (see [[Ether C1]]). 
- Primordials can consume [[Ether C1]] and add it to their bodies, allowing them to grow in [[Ether C1]]-potency and power. However, they can only consume [[Ether C1]] related to their Thesis due to ideological magnetism (see [[Ether C1]]). They don't need to do this unless they enter a [[The True Nature of Existence|realm]] in which their [[Ether C1]] began manifesting and they suddenly need a constant supply. 

**Classifications of Primordials**
- Archaic Theses. Ancient, primal, and simple theses yield primordials with unfathomable [[Ether C1]]-potency, but they're much less intelligent. They perform their function and consume vast amounts of [[Ether C1]]. Some are functionally braindead, unaware of their own existence, and are reduced to nothing but simple algorithms.  
- Complex Theses. Theses that are abstract, nuanced, and unnatural yield primordials with lower [[Ether C1]]-potency, but vast intelligence.
