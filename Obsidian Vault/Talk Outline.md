Body 1: What Is Prophecy
Paragraph Formula:
- Question
- Introduce a scripture
- Explain the scripture
- Illustrate the scripture / Joke
- Make practical value clear
Content
- Prophecy is a message inspired by God: a divine revelation.
- Prophecy doesn't have to involve foretelling the future
- There are many examples of prophecies not foretelling the future

Body 2: How Did Prophecy Occur
Paragraph Formula:
- Question
- Introduce a scripture
- Explain the scripture

- Make practical value clear
Content
- Prophecy occurred through writing, communication from angels, visions, and mental guidance

Conclusion
