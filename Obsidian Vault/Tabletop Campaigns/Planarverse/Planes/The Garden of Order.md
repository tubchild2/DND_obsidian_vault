A universe wide garden city teeming with life. Its ecosystem is blended into the architecture and is wonderfully complex and balanced. It's symmetrical and functional, and everything is balanced and orderly.

Residence of
- The Primordial of Life. Resides at the top of the Garden.
- The Primordial of Flesh. Resides at the bottom of the garden in the Caverns of Viscera.
- The Primordial of Order
- The Primordial of Good
- The Primordial of Peace

Local Species:
- The Gardeners. Tall floating creatures that tend to the ecosystem. They have nature abilities. Created by the Primordial of Life.
- The Sentinels. Tall perfectly symmetrical creatures capable of creating portals between two points in the multiverse to enforce order. Created by the Primordial of Order.
- Amalgams. Chaotic jumbles of flesh, legs, and mouths. Created by the Primordial of Flesh.
- Good. Small doughy humanoids without an aggressive bone in their bodies. They behave perfectly all the time. Created by the Primordial of Good and the Primordial of Peace.
