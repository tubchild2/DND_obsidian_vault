**THE PLANE OF FIRE**
Contains an unending amount of perpetually burning fire. There's no water, and everything flammable is immediately consumed here.

Residence of 
- The Primordial of Fire
- The Primordial of Heat

Local Species
- Fire Elementals. Creatures made of pure fire that seek to burn things to grow in power. They are powerful, and all they want is more power and heat.



**THE PLANE OF WATER**
Contains an unending amount of water. It's literally just a universe wide ocean. 

Residence of
- The Primordial of Water. 
- The Primordial of Cold.

Local Species
- Water Elementals. Creatures made of pure water that seek calm still water to reside in. They don't do much, and they adapt quite quickly. They want things to remain the same.



**THE PLANE OF AIR**
Contains a vast cloud-filled sky of just air and floating objects. 

Residence of 
- The Primordial of Air

Local Species
- Air Elementals. Creatures made of wind, clouds, and smoke that seek storms to grow in size and power. They're wild and unpredictable and they're always on the move.



**THE PLANE OF EARTH**
The entire universe is filled with gigantic caverns, some of which are large enough to fit entire mountain ranges. It's all rocks and minerals.

Residence of
- The Primordial of Earth
- The Primordial of Substance

Local Species
- Earth Elementals. Creatures made of metals and minerals that seek rare metals and gemstones to consume to grow in size and rarity. They're very greedy.
